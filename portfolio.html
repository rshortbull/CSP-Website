Python Indiana Banana

In my Python game Indiana Banana, where a minion enters a temple to retrieve the Golden Banana, I implemented 2D platformer movement with simulated gravity and collision detection. I also developed a simple level editor using multiple functions, which allows for quick and easy level creation, contributing to a longer and more engaging gameplay experience. Additionally, I programmed moving turtles as dynamic obstacles, adding an extra layer of challenge. To enhance the narrative, I used different backgrounds to help tell the story throughout the game.

Python E Card

In our Python E-Card, we crafted an intriguing story about a UFO abducting various animals and people from a farm, leading to chaos and mystery. The narrative follows a child's father as he investigates his child's disappearance and uncovers the UFO's presence. To celebrate the obscure National Alien Abduction Day, we designed a spooky nighttime farm scene inspired by Halloween. The hand-animated farm ends in an engaging cutscene, enhancing the overall storytelling in Python.
